How to Use the CCU Patient Dashboard

1. View Patient Data

To view patient data, go to the "View Patient Data" section from the dashboard. Here you can see a detailed list of all patients.

2. Upload Patient Data

To upload patient data, go to the "Upload Patient Data" section. You will need to upload a file containing patient information. Make sure the data is in the correct format. After uploading, the data will be processed and added to the system.

3. Analyse Patient Data

This page provides tools to generate insights from patient data. The system automatically calculates averages and identifies patients with missing or incomplete data.

4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What formats are supported for uploading patient data?

    The system currently supports CSV and Excel formats.

  • How do I analyze the data?

    After you load patient data, you can view trends, averages, and statistics in the "Analyze Patient Data" section.

  • How can I contact support?

    If you need help or encounter any issues, please reach out to us via email at utkudemirel07@hotmail.com.

5. Additional Support

For additional questions or technical support, please refer to the documentation available on the website or contact our support team.
